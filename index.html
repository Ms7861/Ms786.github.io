About Coast to Coast Commercial

Coast to Coast Commercial is a premier UK commercial real estate advisory firm, established in 2025 to provide exceptional property solutions across every dimension of the commercial market. With a team of experienced surveyors, negotiators and property managers, we offer unrivalled local knowledge combined with national reach.

Our core business encompasses the sale and letting of offices, retail units, industrial spaces and development land across the United Kingdom. From prime London offices to regional industrial estates, we advise investors, landlords and occupiers with integrity, market insight and professionalism.

We are proud to be an independent firm, putting client relationships at the heart of everything we do. Our approach combines traditional values of trust and transparency with modern technology and marketing to achieve outstanding results for our clients.

All members of our team are RICS-regulated and bound by the highest professional standards. We are members of The Property Ombudsman scheme, ensuring complete peace of mind for every client.

Our commitment to compliance and professional standards

Property Misdescriptions Act

We comply fully with the Property Misdescriptions Act 1991 and Consumer Protection Regulations. All property particulars are for guidance only and intended to give a fair description of the property. Prospective purchasers and tenants should verify all details independently.

Commercial Leases

All commercial leases are governed by the Landlord and Tenant Act 1954 (Part II) unless explicitly contracted out. We strongly recommend all parties seek independent legal advice before entering any commercial lease agreement.

Data Protection (UK GDPR)

We process personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation and Data Protection Act 2018. Your information is used solely for property-related communications and never shared with third parties without explicit consent. Full privacy policy available on request.

Money Laundering Regulations

As a regulated property firm, we comply with the Money Laundering, Terrorist Financing and Transfer of Funds Regulations 2017. Proof of identity and address is required for all successful purchasers and tenants prior to transaction completion.

Energy Performance Certificates

EPCs are provided for all commercial properties as required by the Energy Performance of Buildings (England and Wales) Regulations 2012. All EPC ratings displayed are current and valid. Full certificates available on request.

Complaints Procedure

We are members of The Property Ombudsman scheme (membership no: TPO1234). Our full complaints procedure is available on request. In the event of a dispute, clients may contact The Property Ombudsman for independent resolution.
